TECHNOLOGY DECISIONS
================================================================================

Framework: FastAPI
  Why: Modern, async, auto-docs, type-safe, fast

Database: SQLite
  Why: Simple setup, portable, perfect for demo
  Production: Migrate to PostgreSQL (guide provided)

ORM: SQLAlchemy
  Why: Industry standard, database-agnostic, powerful

AI: OpenAI GPT-4o-mini
  Why: Cost-effective, fast, reliable, sufficient
  Cost: $0.01-0.02 per query (500-1000 queries in budget)

Validation: Pydantic
  Why: Type-safe, auto-validation, FastAPI integration
